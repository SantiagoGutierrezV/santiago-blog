About Me

This is who I am.

I am Santiago Gutierrez. I am a student, an employee, and a developer. I attend Hallmark University, a local private school in my hometown of San Antonio, Texas. I am pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Information Systems. I am employed at Oak Hills Church as a Help Desk Technician where I get to help other people and grow my skill set. When not at work or at school, I am most often keeping myself busy by not only creating software, but learning about it too. I have used a wide range of tools such as Python, JavaScript, Swift, HTML, CSS, and several frameworks.

While going to school and working, I realized that I knew much more about networking, cybersecurity, scripting, and even communicating with others than just a couple of months prior. I decided to pursue several certifications and can now happily say that I am CompTIA A+, Network+, and Security+ certified. And I am in the process of obtaining my CCNA and AWS Cloud Practitioner certifications. I have also taken the time to become a member of ISSA (Information Systems Security Association), an organization made up of some of the most intelligent cybersecurity experts and IT specialists.

My passion lies in software development. I find it fascinating uncovering the power of computing and how I can use code to wield that power. I have loved every moment of my journey that started in the fourth grade when I was programming Lego robots, to high school where I was programming Arduino microcontrollers, to now where I am developing more complicated web applications and projects that have practical uses. I dream to be able to use everything I have learned (and haven't learned) in a career as a software engineer.
